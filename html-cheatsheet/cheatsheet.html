Semantic HTML
Header Used for H1, Nav <header></header> Includes H1, Nav, Hero/Jumbotron. Also can be 1 per section per w3c.
Main Used for Main Content, not DOM related <main></main> Should be Unique per page; not contain repeated across a set of documents/content; nav, sidebars, copyright, etc.
Section Used for whole sections of the webpage <section></section> May be several parts
aside Used for whole sections of the webpage <aside></aside> The HTML <aside> element represents a portion of a document whose content is only indirectly related to the document's main content. Asides are frequently presented as sidebars or call-out boxes.
article Used for independent content <article></article>
footer Used for repeated footer <foort></footer> May involve 2nd Nav, copyright.
